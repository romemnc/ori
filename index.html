Origami is the Japanese art of paper folding , with "ori" meaning folding and "kami" meaning paper.

One of the most iconic origami creations is the paper crane. It's a classic origami design that represents elegance, hope, peace, and longevity.

Are you ready to fold? Then, let’s get started!

FUN FACTS ABOUT PAPER CRANES

Fact#01

In Japanese culture, the paper crane is a symbol of hope, healing, and peace.

Fact#02

Folding 1,000 paper cranes is believed to fulfill wishes.

Fact#03

Paper cranes are one of the most iconic and widely recognized origami creations.

Fact#04

Paper crane has become a symbol of peace because of a Japanese girl named Sadako Sasaki.
